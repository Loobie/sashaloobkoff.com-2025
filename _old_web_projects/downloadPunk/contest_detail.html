Contest Description

If you want to hear that MOTION CITY SOUNDTRACK is made up of ex-members of some formerly famous groups, forget it. Unless the names of their high school bands mean anything to you, who they are and where they came from will ring no familiar tones, until now. As for the approach to the song writing process, Justin gives this perspective, "I think of a persons head as a projector that is constantly viewing and creating home movies of their past, present and future. Not necessarily what happened, but how they remember or envision things happening, a combination of reality and daydream. Lyrically, I really like those that stay with you over time, lyrics that haunt you either in a good or a bad way.
