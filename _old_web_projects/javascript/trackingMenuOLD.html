Tuesday, October 15th, 2002



Brazil!

Samiam are back home from their hugely successfull Brazillian tour and we have photos! A big thank you to Cesar, Alexandre, Marlisi, Jose, Antionio, Francesco and everyone that sent me their shots!

But before you check out the photos, read Sean's Brazillian Tour diary in "The Band" section of the site!

Sean also sent me the url of his new band: The Fakers. I heard a demo a couple months ago and it was really good. Sean sings and plays guitar.

Earlier I suggested that Jason might've jammed with The Fakers a couple of times on a recent NYC visit. I was wrong. Sean and Jason did work on some songs, but they were Samiam songs. Can't wait to hear some new stuff (by both bands).

Anybody like The Vines? I thought I did based on their single (which rocks) but I bought the cd and it's pretty disappointing. Not terrible or anything but not great either. Anyways, the only reason I bring it up is that VIc (Samiam's Clumsy-era drummer) played drums on one of the songs on the album. The man gets around.

Smitty wrote in to tell us that Samiam came in #8 in the new BlankTV top ten list. Right on. If you have a speedy connection, check out some videos!

A New Song!

Well, it ain't so new... but it's previously unreleased.

Hopeless Record's new comp, "Hopelessly Devoted to You, Vol. 4," features a song from the "Astray" sessions entitled "Look No Hand." Great song, I think it should have made the cd. It would've been one of my favorites. Click here for a 30 sec mp3 sample.

Anyways, you can pre-order the cd from Hopeless for only $2. Not bad. Also featured are tracks by Thrice, Common Rider, The Weakerthans and the usual cast of Hopeless characters. Oh, and if your copy contains the winning ticket, you win a free Dell computer. Yippeee.

From what I hear, the shows in May went well. I got a million emails from people wanting to get on the guest list for the SF show, so I know people were excited. Unfortunately, Samiam were added to the bill after the show was sold out so few Samiam fans actually got in. Sergie got plastered and tore up the club... so all is well.

No more shows are booked at the moment, so please save your emails.

In Other News:

James will be hocking copies by mail. You can get ordering info here.
